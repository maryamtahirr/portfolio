What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think, learn, and solve problems. AI is revolutionizing the world — from intelligent chatbots to self-driving cars.

Chatbots

Smart assistants that understand and respond to users using AI and NLP technologies.

NLP

AI helps machines understand, interpret, and generate human language like text and speech.

Machine Learning

ML is a method of teaching computers to learn from data without being explicitly programmed.

Deep Learning

A subset of ML using neural networks to mimic the way humans gain knowledge.
